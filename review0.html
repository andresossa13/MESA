10 tips to choose your favourite indie game

By chat gpt

Mon 19 april 2023

13.00 PM GMT +2

GET TICKETS

Indie games have grown in popularity over the years, with more and more games being released every year. These games often offer unique gameplay experiences and tell interesting stories that are not found in mainstream games. However, with so many indie games to choose from, it can be difficult to know where to start. In this article, we will provide you with ten tips to help you choose your favourite indie game.

  1. Look for recommendations from friends and family

    2. One of the easiest ways to find a great indie game is to ask your friends and family for recommendations. Chances are, you know someone who enjoys playing indie games and can provide you with some excellent suggestions. Ask them which games they have enjoyed playing and why.

  2. Read reviews from trusted sources

    3. Another great way to find an indie game that you will love is to read reviews from trusted sources. Look for reviews from websites or individuals that have similar tastes in games to you. This will help you get a better idea of what to expect from the game before you purchase it.

  3. Check out gameplay footage on YouTube

    4. Watching gameplay footage on YouTube is an excellent way to get a better idea of what to expect from an indie game. Many YouTubers play through entire games, providing you with a good understanding of what the gameplay is like and whether it is something you would enjoy.

  4. Consider the game's genre

    5. When looking for an indie game, it is essential to consider the game's genre. Indie games cover a wide range of genres, from action-adventure to puzzle games. Consider which genres you enjoy the most and look for indie games that fit within those categories.

  5. Look for games with unique gameplay mechanics

    6. One of the things that make indie games stand out from mainstream games is their unique gameplay mechanics. Look for games that offer something different, such as a unique control scheme or a different take on a genre you enjoy.

  6. Consider the game's art style

    7. Indie games often feature unique art styles that set them apart from mainstream games. Consider whether the game's art style is something you enjoy and whether it adds to the overall experience.

  7. Look for games with compelling narratives

    8. Many indie games feature compelling narratives that draw you in and keep you engaged throughout the game. Look for games that tell interesting stories and have well-developed characters.

  8. Consider the game's length

    9. Indie games can vary greatly in length, from short games that can be completed in a few hours to longer games that take dozens of hours to complete. Consider how much time you have to play games and look for games that fit within your schedule.

  9. Look for games with replayability

    10. Some indie games offer excellent replayability, with multiple endings, procedurally generated levels, or other features that make each playthrough unique. Consider whether the game offers something that will keep you coming back for more.

  10. Support independent developers

    11. Finally, when choosing an indie game, consider supporting independent developers. Many indie games are created by small teams or even individuals, and supporting these developers helps them continue to create unique and innovative games.

In conclusion, choosing your favourite indie game can be challenging, but by following these ten tips, you can find a game that you will love. Remember to consider the game's genre, art style, narrative, length, and replayability, and don't forget to support independent developers.
